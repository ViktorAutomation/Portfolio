Featured Build · Insurance

Operating System for a US Property Insurance Company

A US property insurance company was running daily operations across spreadsheets, manual email reminders, separate signing tools, and disconnected billing — with no single place to see where a customer stood across the policy lifecycle.

I designed and built a complete Monday.com operating system that unifies the entire lifecycle — new customer onboarding, applications, inspections, smart home equipment activation, mortgagee changes, billing, claims, renewals, and cancellations — connected through Make to Hubflo (customer portal + Smartdocs e-signature), QuickBooks Online (billing), Box (signed document repository), and Slack (escalation alerts).

Monday is the system of record; the Monday Customer Item ID acts as the immutable identifier across every connected platform, so when emails, addresses, or policy numbers change, every record stays linked end-to-end. Reminder cadences, escalation logic, red-flag detection, and audit-ready document trails are owned by Monday — not buried in inboxes.
